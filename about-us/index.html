About Us

Our Story

Passion for meaningful change

Our passion for creating meaningful change for patients is what sets us apart. The iFocus team believes in the potential of our idea, and it truly shows in all of our work. We work tirelessly to bring a better tomorrow, and we're proud of every single member of our team.

Our Mission

Empowering better treatment decisions

iFocus Health empowers patients and caregivers with data-driven insights for informed ADHD treatment decisions. Our cutting-edge technology relieves the burden of decision-making, omits the guesswork, and leads to better outcomes for people with ADHD. Our mission is to transform ADHD treatment with an innovative, effective, and easy-to-use tool.
